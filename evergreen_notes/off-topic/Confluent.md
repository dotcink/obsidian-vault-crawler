Confluent develops and offers services around {Apache Kafka} (a {real-time stream processing platform}).

In {January 2019}, they raised a {$125M} Series {D} on a {$2.4B} post-money valuation. At that time, their annual revenue was {~$100M}; they were not yet profitable.

As of October 2020 they were 1,300 people.
