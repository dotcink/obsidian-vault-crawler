# E-reading popularity sharply trails all book-reading

In [a 2018 Pew survey](https://www.pewresearch.org/fact-tank/2019/09/25/one-in-five-americans-now-listen-to-audiobooks/), the plurality of respondents read print books only. About 3/4 of respondents read any book in the past year; only 1/4 read an e-book in the past year. The trend is flat-to-downwards. But audiobooks are on the rise.

Possible cause: [[Reading texts on computers is unpleasant]].
